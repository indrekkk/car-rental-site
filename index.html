About Exoticar car rental

Lotus Car Rental Iceland is a discount car rental company in Iceland. We provide reliable and affordable 4x4 vehicles, vans and compact cars. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, our friendly staff provides the best in customer service so that visitors make the most out of their trip to Iceland. With unlimited mileage and no hidden fees, our fleet of cars has something for everyone!

Our head office is located at Keflavik International Airport area and we offer free transport to and from the airport. We also offer a pick-up and drop off service in Reykjavik City and Keflavik Town for a small additional fee.
